Hello! I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at the Simons Laufer Mathematical Sciences Institute (formerly MSRI). Prior to this, I completed my PhD in Probability Theory from Eindhoven University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Remco van der Hofstad and Dr. Rajat Subhra Hazra. My doctoral thesis is titled "Stochastic Processes on Preferential Attachment Models".

I am working on Random Graphs. Besides this, my areas of interest are Stochastic Process, Random Interfaces, Random Walks, Statistical Physics, and Applied Probability Theory.
