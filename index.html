In short,

Hello! I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at the Division of Applied Mathematics, Brown University, in the group of Prof. Kavita Ramanan. Prior to this, I completed my PhD in Probability theory from Eindhoven University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Remco van der Hofstad and Dr. Rajat Subhra Hazra. My doctoral thesis is titled "Stochastic Processes on Preferential Attachment Models".

I am working on Interacting particle systems on random graphs. Besides this, my area of interests are Stochastic process, Interacting particle systems, Random interfaces, Random walks, Statistical physics.
