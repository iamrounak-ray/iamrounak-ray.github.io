In short,

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at the Division of Applied Mathematics, Brown University, in the group of Prof. Kavita Ramanan. Prior to this, I completed my PhD in Probability theory from Eindhoven University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Remco van der Hofstad and Dr. Rajat Subhra Hazra. My doctoral thesis is titled "Stochastic Processes on Preferential Attachment Models".

I am working on Interacting particle systems on random graphs. Besides this, my area of interests are Stochastic process, Interacting particle systems, Random interfaces, Random walks, Statistical physics.

Here is something other than my academics. I love to play table tennis, chess, football (though I am terribly bad at this). I like to read comics and bengali storybooks. And I love travelling. I want to see the whole world. I am a foodie also. I like to taste the special food items of every place I visit.
